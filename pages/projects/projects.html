To better understanding and retaining in my mind each technology and topic, I think the best way to do it is dividing my learning in two parts.

In the first hours of learned a complete topic or a part of it (depends on the complexity) :

  1. Make 3 Webpages/websites in a row with what I learned (surely reviewing the source).
  2. Keep going with the next topic.
  3. Adding the new topics to the first websites to practice.
  4. Make 2 Webpages/websites from scratch, joining all.

Passed 8 hours or a day, starting the day with the making of 2 webpages from scratch with the learned the previous day. This will strengthen my memory and improve my practice.
